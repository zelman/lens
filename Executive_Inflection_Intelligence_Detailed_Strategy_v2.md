# Executive Inflection Intelligence (EII)

A high-trust, high-signal subscription for experienced operators targeting **Seed–Series A** inflection roles **before they’re posted**.

## Context and Constraint
- Constraint: ramp to **~$100k income quickly**.
- Implication: avoid low-ARPU, slow-ramp consumer SaaS. Use **premium operator pricing** + **direct outbound** + **manual/semi-automated delivery** first.

---

## WHY (Problem)
Experienced CS/RevOps/GTM operators don’t need better resumes — they need a way to find the **small set** of companies at the exact break point where:
- enterprise motion is emerging,
- sales is ramping,
- leadership gaps exist,
- and roles often **aren’t posted yet**.

Existing tools are reactive:
- **Job boards**: roles after posting.
- **LinkedIn**: snapshots, not forecasts.
- **Crunchbase**: funding events, not functional sequencing.

EII forecasts **what the company will need next**.

---

## WHAT (Deliverables)
**Weekly Inflection Radar**
- Top 25–50 companies
- **30/60/90-day role likelihood**
- Reason codes: *why now*

**Watchlist + alerts**
- headcount velocity, leadership additions, enterprise cues

**Outreach pack**
- suggested angles, founder/context snippets, “why you / why now” framing

**Founding cohort**
- light community + feedback loop

---

## HOW (Signals + Lens)
Signals (interpretable, reason-coded):
- Funding recency + stage
- Headcount velocity (overall + sales ramp)
- Leadership gaps (no CS/RevOps leader)
- Enterprise cues (pricing/security/SSO/SOC2)
- Customer proof velocity (logos/case studies)

**Lens-driven**: encodes operator philosophy (builder roles, disqualifiers, thresholds).

---

## Competitive Positioning (Quadrant)
Axes:
- X: **Reactive → Predictive**
- Y: **Generic → Functional Inflection**

Examples:
- Crunchbase / LinkedIn: reactive + generic
- Recruiter tools: reactive + functional (sourcing), not forecasting
- **EII**: predictive + functional inflection forecasting

Positioning line:
> Crunchbase tells you what happened. LinkedIn shows you who’s there. Job boards show you what’s posted. **We forecast what a company will need next.**

## Business Model (Designed for “income fast”)
Pricing:
- Founding cohort: **$99–$149/mo** (target **$125**), locked for 12 months
- Later: $149–$199/mo standard plan
- Optional concierge add-on: $300–$600/mo (limited)

Revenue targets:
- 40 @ $125 = $5k MRR (~$60k ARR)
- 100 @ $125 = $12.5k MRR (~$150k ARR)
- 200 @ $125 = $25k MRR (~$300k ARR)

Costs (lean):
- Data/LLM/API: $500–$2,000/mo early (cache + dedup is critical)
- Tools: $200–$800/mo
- No full-time engineers until retention + MRR justify

## 30/60/90/120-Day Plan

### Days 0–30: Pre-sell + First Value
- Define persona lens v1 (CS/CX leader) + disqualifiers
- Create sample report + landing page
- Build list of 150 operators; outbound to first 100
- Close 25–40 founding members
- Deliver first paid report (manual)

### Days 31–60: Retention + Repeatability
- Ship weekly radar on fixed cadence
- Add lightweight watchlist alerts
- Grow to 75–100 members (referrals + wave 2 outbound)
- Publish 4 public “inflection memos”

### Days 61–90: Automation + Outcome Loop
- Add trajectory table (weekly deltas)
- Add outcome tracking (role posted/hire event detected)
- Refine scoring weights monthly + reason codes
- Target 125–150 members; test $149 for new joins
- Prototype second lens with 10–20 beta users

### Days 91–120: Scale Engine
- Launch second lens; bundle tiers
- Improve onboarding (lens intake/wizard)
- Add monthly office hours to reduce churn
- Target 150–200 members
- Decide expansion: multi-lens B vs VC-lens C upsell

---

## KPIs
- Acquisition: reply %, calls booked, close rate, referrals/member
- Activation: report opens, watchlist engagement
- Value: intros, interviews, conversations started
- Retention: month-1 churn, month-2 retention

---

## Risks & Mitigations
- Churn: track wins, tighten report quality, reason codes
- Data fragility: prefer public/first-party sources; cache/dedup
- No audience: outbound + referrals + weekly public memos
- Overbuilding: keep v1 manual; automate after retention
