## GLOBAL CHANGES (apply to all three decks)

### G1. Tagline / Subtitle Pivot

| Deck | Current | New |
|------|---------|-----|
| lens-intro (slide 1) | "AI-Powered Professional Identity Discovery" | "Search-Critical Discovery & Alignment for Executive Search" |
| lens-jamie-stern (slide 1) | "Signal matching over keyword matching" | "Prevent expensive misalignment in high-stakes executive search" |
| lens-investor-pitch (slide 1) | "We replace resumes with a structured identity system..." | "We help executive search firms win and de-risk high-stakes placements through structured discovery and alignment" |

### G2. Oh/Wang/Mount Reframe (all research slides)

**Current language (all decks):** "Oh, Wang & Mount (2011) found that observer ratings predict job performance better than self-reports — the exact foundation for coached discovery over surveys."

**New language:** "Oh, Wang & Mount (2011) showed observer ratings of personality predict job performance better than self-reports. This research *inspired* Lens's approach: if extraction depth matters, coached conversational discovery should produce richer, more structured signal than standardized self-report instruments. Lens produces higher-resolution structured self-report today; our roadmap includes external observer signals (references, peer input) to move toward genuine observer-grade validity."

**Key change:** "exact foundation" → "inspired." Remove any claim that the AI conversation IS observer-informed. It's inspired by that research, not validated by it.

### G3. Competitive Landscape Honesty

**Current claim (all decks):** "Nobody does deep discovery on both sides" / "Nobody occupies the deep + scalable quadrant"

**New claim:** "Others match on behavioral signals using standardized self-report instruments (Plum, Torre.ai, Jack & Jill). Lens is differentiated by input method (coached conversational extraction), output format (portable, user-owned document), and market focus ($300K+ executive search)."

**Add competitors to tables:**
- Plum — behavioral assessment + role matching, standardized forced-choice, high-volume focus, $750/mo
- Torre.ai — 112-factor "professional genome" matching, $10M seed, mid-market/LATAM focus
- Acknowledge Jack & Jill does 20-min conversational intake (shallow vs. 45-min coaching depth)

### G4. Role Lens First Resequencing

**Current flow (all decks):** Discover candidate → Produce lens → Match to opportunities

**New flow:** Define role (stakeholder alignment) → Discover candidate → Score both → Client-facing deliverable

Every slide showing the product flow needs to lead with the role lens as Phase 1.

### G5. Value Prop Language Shift

| Current | New |
|---------|-----|
| "Capture who someone actually is" | "Prevent expensive misalignment in high-stakes searches" |
| "A lens, not a resume" | "The structured discovery and alignment layer your ATS doesn't have" |
| "Deeper identity-based matching" | "Win searches, save time, show rigor" |
| "Signal matching over keyword matching" | "Structured discovery that helps firms win and de-risk C-suite searches" |
| "Nobody does both sides with depth" | "Others assess with surveys. We discover through coaching." |

### G6. Quadrant Slide Update

Add Plum and Torre.ai to the "Assessments" cluster (bottom-right area). Keep the "Every tool optimizes the search. None improves the input." tagline — it still holds, just needs the honest caveat about input method differentiation.
